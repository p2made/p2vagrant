p2v Initialisation

Before using your VM, certain software needs to be installed...

  * Homebrew (https://brew.sh) package manager
  * vagrant
  * vagrant-vmware-utility

Vagrant plugins...

  * vagrant-share
  * vagrant-vmware-desktop

Additional packages...

  * yq      - for saving preferences and application state
  * zenity  - for GUI functionality

Optionally a small desktop app that you may find useful...

  * vagrant-manager

Enjoy your p2vagrant VM.
